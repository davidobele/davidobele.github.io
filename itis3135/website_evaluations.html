CRAP Website Discovery and Evaluation

Good Website: Apple

Apple.com has good website design provides a good user experience. The site has a clean responsive design that is very visually appealing. The products displayed on the website are organized and grouped together and the colors used provide good contrast

Contrast: Uses high contrast between text and backgrounds, clear color differentiation, and distinct text

Repetition: Consistent use of white space, uniform grid layout, and consistent design across pages

Alignment: Precise grid-based layout with clear, content is aligned and structured

Proximity: Related elements are grouped together, clear separation between different content sections

Bad Website: Craigslist

Craigslist's website looks very old and outdated. The page layout is very text heavy and links are packed together with bad spacing. The colors on the webpage could also improve for better visability

CRAP Principles

Contrast: Low color contrast, minimal visual differentiation, plain text-heavy design

Repetition: Inconsistent formatting, no unified design system, varying text styles and sizes

Alignment: Unstructured layout, left-aligned text with no clear visual hierarchy, cluttered appearance

Proximity: No clear grouping of related elements, dense text with minimal white space
